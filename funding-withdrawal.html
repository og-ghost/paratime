Funding and withdrawals

It is simple and straightforward to deposit and withdraw funds to and from your OATREX trading account. Funds can be deposited using debit card (backed by MasterCard, Visa or Discover), bank transfer, Bitcoin and Perfect Money. You can withdraw funds using debit card, bank wire transfer and bitcoin.

Funding

To initiate your first deposit into your OANDA trading account, simply log in to 'manage funds' using your OATREXA account details.

Debit card

We accept debit cards backed by MasterCard, Visa and Discover. There is a USD$20,000 maximum limit per calendar month.

Bank wire transfer

It takes one to three business days to deposit funds via domestic wire transfer and up to five business days for international wire transfers. There is no minimum or maximum deposit limit. You may be charged a service fee by your bank and intermediary bank for transferring funds into your OATREXA trading account.

Bitcoin

To deposit funds to your account via bitcoin, simply click on "Deposit" when logged in to your account. Fill out the amount you will like to deposit in the base currency on your account and submit your request. You should get presented a QR Code you can scan directly from your bitcoin wallet and make your deposit. Deposits via bitcoin take only a few miniutes to get processed to your account and you get a higher welcome bonus on deposits via bitcoin

Perfect Money

Deposit via Perfect money takes only a few minutes to get processed to your trading account with minimal processing fee on your transfer. However, this method of deposit is not available to residents of the United States

Third-party deposits: to receive funds from a third party, you will first need to set up a oint account with that party. Select the “instantly add” option to expedite deposits via wire transfer, Bitcoin, Perfect money.

Withdrawals

You can withdraw funds from your OATREX account via debit card, bank wire transfer and Bitcoin. Funds can be withdrawn by logging in to ‘manage funds’ using your OATREX Trade account details. Remember: withdrawals are subject to our hierarchy rule and taxation. If you have deposited funds using multiple methods, you must exhaust the total deposit amounts first by debit card, and then followed by bank wire transfer and bitcoin.

Debit card

It takes up to three business days for funds to be processed into your account. The total amount you can withdraw into your debit card(s) cannot be more than the amount you originally deposited using your debit card(s). Any funds remaining in your account after the original amount has been withdrawn can be taken out using an alternative method, such as a bank wire transfer.

Bank wire transfer

To withdraw funds via bank transfer, your bank account must be in the same name as your OATREX trading account. It takes between one and two business days to withdraw funds via bank wire transfer within the US and up to five business days for international withdrawals. You must confirm that you are the owner of the bank account to which you wish to withdraw funds. You can do this in the form of a wire transfer deposit from the bank account (not all bank transfers can provide required bank details to confirm account ownership), or by submitting documentation. If you send funds via ACH, the fee attached to your withdrawal request will be removed during processing and funds will be sent at no charge.
