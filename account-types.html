What are OATREX's hours of operation?

Our hours of operation coincide with the global financial markets. In the US, trading is available from approximately 5pm Sunday to 5pm Friday (New York time). Please note: these times are subject to change during daylight savings time.

What are the deposit options to fund my OATREX account for forex trading?

Our deposit options vary based on the OATREX division with which you hold your account. Please check the relevant deposit funds section for more details on how to fund your account.

How secure is trading with OATREX

Trading with OATREX is completely secure and protected. OATREX uses the globally recognized SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) security system which encrypts all credit card payments over the web. This system is automatic and fully protected.

Am I required to install any software to trade on OATREX?

There is NO software installation necessary to trade on OATREX.com. The platform is 100% web reliant and can be accessed anywhere in the world.
